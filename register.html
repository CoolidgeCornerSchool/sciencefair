For Grades 6, 7, and 8, the regional science fair will be held on Saturday April 21, 2018.
Up to 10 projects from 6, 7, and 8th grade may be selected to represent Devotion school.

If your research involves human subjects and you would like to represent the Devotion school at the regional fair, you will be required to have informed consent forms from your subjects. The regional fair has strict safety requirements that exclude the use of infectious agents, recombinant DNA and hazardous compounds. Additional information can be found here or by talking with your science teacher or asking the science fair team at devotionsciencefair@gmail.com.
