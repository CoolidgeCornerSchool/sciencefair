Regional Science Fair

For Grades 6, 7, and 8, the regional science fair will be held on Saturday April 6, 2019.
Up to 10 projects from 6, 7, and 8th grade may be selected to represent Coolidge Corner School.

If your research involves human subjects and you would like to represent the Coolidge Corner School at the regional fair, you will be required to have informed consent forms from your subjects. The regional fair has strict safety requirements that exclude the use of infectious agents, recombinant DNA and hazardous compounds. Additional information can be found here, or by talking with your science teacher, or asking Chris Dulla.
